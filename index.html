Useless Tech You Should Never Buy

not every peice of tech deserves buying

The "audiophile SSD" actually upset me. I spent some time browsing the forums where it was talked about and they were all basically drinking some intense koolaid, calling out websites making fun of or otherwise pointing out the absurdity of the claims as stupid or otherwise uninformed garbage. I feel bad for them. I actually genuinely feel bad for them. There were people in there comparing the different kinds of m.2 storage for their audio quality, and pointed out that intel optane was the best in his experience for crystal clear sound. The thought that the storage medium of the ones and zeros used to digitally represent your music somehow modified the sound signature and that it was physically perceptible by your ears is the most ridiculous, batshit insane claim you could ever hope to make. That's like tiers above reptile men ruling over the lower classes in the believability spectrum.
